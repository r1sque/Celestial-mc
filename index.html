What is Minecraft + ?

Minecraft+ is a vanilla-friendly modpack designed to aims to keep the vanilla aspect of minecraft while enhancing the experience. Instead of adding numerous new addon mods, it focuses on reimagining existing structures and mechanics. Additionally, it includes quality-of-life improvements to make gameplay smoother and more enjoyable throughout your adventure.

Minecraft + advantages

As mentioned before, this modpack blends beautiful visuals, performance, customizability, compatibility, and Easy troubleshooting for an enhanced Minecraft experience.

Beautiful appearance

Our client has a large number of visual features that will make your game more colorful. You can also customize the design, making it really beautiful.

Performance

We are constantly improving the optimization of our client and the game itself. You will be able to run our client even on weak computers.

Customizability

In our client, you can customize almost any function for yourself, as well as use the configurations of other users.

Compatibility

We regularly update our client, adding new functions and improving existing ones, giving it advantages over all existing servers.

Easy troubleshooting

Despite its large size, the modpack is very easy to troubleshoot. Made on Fabric, it allows you to track missing mods issues making issue resolution straightforward.

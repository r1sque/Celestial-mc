What is Celestial ?

Celestial is a vanilla-friendly modpack designed to aims to keep the vanilla aspect of minecraft while enhancing the experience. Instead of adding numerous new addon mods, it focuses on reimagining existing structures and mechanics. Additionally, it includes quality-of-life improvements to make gameplay smoother and more enjoyable throughout your adventure.

Celestial Advantages

Our modpack combines stunning visuals, optimized performance, extensive customization options, broad compatibility, and straightforward troubleshooting for an enhanced Minecraft experience.

Stunning Visuals

Experience Minecraft like never before with our enhanced visual features. Customize your game's appearance with a wide range of options to create your perfect aesthetic.

Optimized Performance

Made on a foundation of optimized mods, Celestial runs smoothly on a wide range of systems. Enjoy enhanced gameplay without compromising performance, even on lower-end computers.

Extensive Customization

Tailor your Minecraft experience to your preferences with our comprehensive customization options. From texture packs to shader packs and mod configurations, create your perfect setup.

Broad Compatibility

Designed for seamless integration, our modpack works with most servers. Incompatible mods automatically disable themselves, ensuring a smooth multiplayer experience.

Easy Troubleshooting

Built on Fabric, our modpack makes issue resolution straightforward. The system automatically tracks and reports missing mods, making troubleshooting quick and efficient.

Celestial Client ModPack

  • 1. Download and install the Modrinth App.
  • 2. Open the app and log in with a Minecraft account.
  • 3. Click on the "+" button labeled "Create Instance" in the very left panel.
  • 4. Select "From Files."
  • 5. Choose the downloaded `.mrpack` file from the link below.
  • 6. The modpack will be installed automatically.
  • 7. Launch the game from Modrinth and join the server.

Celestial Server ModPack

  • 1. Download and install the Fabric Server for Minecraft 1.21.1.
  • 2. Run the installer and complete the server installation process.
  • 3. After installation, import the server modpack into Modrinth.
  • 4. Navigate to the root directory of the Modrinth server profile.
  • 5. Copy the Mods, Config, and Script folders into your MC server directory.
  • 6. Set up the server and configure the mods as needed.
  • 7. Start the server and connect to it to begin playing.
